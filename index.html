Who We Are

Nick and Alston are both trainers based in Nashville Tennessee that have a passion for helping others. With years of experience, the goal of the podcast is to share their training experiences in an effort to bring more people to the world they love. You’ll hear training dos (and donts) along with relatable stories from training, hunting, and the lifestyle in general.
